What is Restruction?

Restruction is an app developed for the construction industry to post and find items or materials that are perfectly good to be reused but have no home to go to. Rather than let these items go to waste, we aim on finding a new home for them through our app.

Our aim is to reduce the vast carbon footprint produced by the construction industry (40% of the entire world's carbon) by saving one salvagable item at a time from going to waste. Register via the button at the top right and join the movement if you work within the construction industry and have items to post or are in search of salvageable items.

Have a browse around our site and below you will find the 10 most recent posts so you can get an understanding of what restruction offers.
